2. How to SLASummit:

When you arrive at the Engineering Common Room in the McConnell Engineering Basement (3480 University Street) on Thursday you will be greeted by our friendly faces and a welcome package. In this package you will have all the information you need for the rest of the conference: your team members, your assigned community, and important locations and times. Teams will consist of five people and you will be assigned a volunteer to guide you through out the next four days.

Friday morning it is very important be on time, the conference will begin with our speaker series promptly at 9:00 am. For more information on our amazing speakers visit: http://slasummit.com/SLASummit2015/SLASummit.html#conference

Dress code through out the entire conference is business casual (excluding pub crawl and Sunday brunch).

3. Community Reports and Grading Rubrics:

I’ve also attached the community reports and the grading rubric. The grading rubric is what the judges will be using to assess your project and eventually chose the winner. The reports include a description of the communities we are looking to help. You will be assigned one out of the three randomly.
